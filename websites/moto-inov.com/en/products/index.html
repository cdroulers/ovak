The now available Ovak seat is the result of research and development going back to May 1997. Its creator, an inveterat motorcyclist since age 16, reaffirms the saying «Need creates invention».

Indeed, being a father later in life, he had to find a way to reconcile his love of motorcycling and snowmobiling with his child aboard. There was no way he would use a harness, side-car or a light car. He wanted to keep his child right beside him on the vehicle without having to tie her to himself.

Since its very early prototypes, the Ovak seat's objective was to comfortably secure the passenger allowing the latter to free himself in case of impact. The following chronology explains its development.
