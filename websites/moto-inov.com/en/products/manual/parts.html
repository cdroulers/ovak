1. Exterior shell is fixed to the motorcycle with six (6) adjustable straps.

M01 Model / Fiberglass

M02 Model / Thermoplastic
2. Retention/ejection inside fiberglass shell covered with velvet cushions and the adjustable retention harness for the passenger.
3. Four adjustable harnessing straps pre-assembled with vinyl fasteners.
4. Four retention straps with protective sheaths that can replace the fastening straps on certain models of vehicles which are attached to the motorcycle frame under the back of the seat.
5. Installation kit of the gauging elements for the retention/ejection system Velcro.
6. Closed cell foam used to balance the front inclination on certain motorcycle seats.
7. Optional: 2 stainless steel fastening bases for snowmobile.
8. Optional: back sack Ovak dual use with reflective surface that can be fixed to the back off the seat.
9. Optional: Dust cover for the seat.
10. Optional: Four seasons cover with a Polar lining for the passenger, which can be attached to the ejector shell.
