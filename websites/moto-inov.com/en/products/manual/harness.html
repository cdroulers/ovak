This requires that the shells be separated to have access to the fasteners under the ejector seat.


Step 1

Is done with steel fasteners on the back portion of the ejector shell. The adjustment of the fasteners under the seat will be easier if you remove partially the cushion from the seat. Reenter the ends of the extra straps in the slots, towards the inside of the shell, under the cushion. It is very important that all surfaces made of Velcro be free of all obstacles.


Step 2

This is done using the upper plastic fasteners in the front (picture 2). Leave the straps loose enough for the passenger.
