The Ovak seat cannot guarantee the absolute protection against injuries. However, the proper use of this seat should reduce the risk of serious injuries or the death of your passenger.

Never leave your passenger unattended.

• The Ovak ejector system is adjusted for a passenger weighing between 25 and 100 pounds. If the passenger is heavier, the ejector system is no longer efficient and the use of the harness becomes useless.

• Beware that your passenger may sustain injuries even if you are not involved in an accident. Comment events like an abrupt breaking or turn can cause the fall of your passenger if the Ovak is not installed properly or if your passenger is not safely tied in the seat.

• Always use the straps of the harness when riding with a young passenger and above all never leave him or her unattended in the seat when the vehicle is parked. A motorcycle can lose its stability and fall because of the passenger's abrupt movements.

Some vehicles are not compatible with the Ovak seat due to their shape or the type of saddle that will not permit to intall the seat safely.

• Only use the original parts, other parts do not necessarily have the resistance to ensure the safety characterizing your Ovak.

CONTACT MOTO INOV INTERNATIONAL TO OBTAIN ADDITIONAL OR REPLACEMENT PARTS.

• Some of the parts of the seat can become very hot if the seat is exposed to the sun, you can sustain burns if touched. Put a cover on the seat when exposed to the sun and always verify the metal and plastic parts before you sit your passenger into it.

• When you do not use the seat, do not put heavy objects on it. This could alter its shape.



RECOMMENDATIONS

Do not alter your seat or add other components that are not ours. Always use the original Ovak parts. Only do the adjustments recommended by Moto Inov International.

Being fabricated with similar products as motorcycle or snowmobile helmets , when any accident occur, damages like small cracks hard to detect could render the seat unsafe, we recommend that you replace it.



CAUTION

Some locations on your vehicle are not safe for this seat. In fact, some vehicles do not have a safe place to install the seat.

An inadequate location to install the seat increases the risk of serious injuries and even death of the passenger.

Follow the instructions in section 5 in order to find the proper location to install the seat. If you are in doubt and do not know where to install the seat on the vehicle please contact the maker (Moto Inov International) 1-877-546-9090 in Acton Vale.
