8. TIPS FOR MAINTENANCE OF THE OVAK SEAT

Your seat is fabricated with first quality products and should give you many years of satisfaction. However, as for your motorcycle or snowmobile, you must take care of it to keep it in its "as new" look. Clean and wash the exterior shell with automotive soap.

The fabric used for the cushion is made with auto material treated with Scotch Guard. You can remove it and wash it by hand with mild soap. Suspend it to dry, never use the dryer. The straps can also be washed with a mild soap and rinsed thoroughly.


Most of all never use a product like "Armor All" on the straps, that could result in the reduction of efficiency of the retention metal fasteners.
