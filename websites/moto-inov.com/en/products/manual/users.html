1. Do not install or use the Ovak seat without having thoroughly read and understood the instructions contained in this manual.

2. Do not let anybody else install or use the Ovak seat unless the person fully understands its functions.

3. The improper installation of the Ovak seat can cause serious injuries and will not meet the goal for which it was conceived.

4. When the vehicle on which the Ovak seat is stopped, the passenger may by his or her movements destabilize the vehicle and make it overturn. Never let a passenger in the Ovak seat unattended (especially a young one).

5. Motorcycles and snowmobiles generate a high level of vibrations and that is why it is most important to verify the state and tension of the harnessing straps every time you drive with a passenger in the Ovak seat.

6. The wear and tear (ripping, wearing) of the harnessing straps can render the use of the Ovak seat unsafe.

Change right away any part showing signs of wear with new parts Ovak.

7. Make sure that the retention harness is well adjusted. Always keep in mind that the ejector inside shell is an additional protection in case of impact.

8. Do not lend, give or sell your Ovak seat to another user without giving him or her this instructions manual.
