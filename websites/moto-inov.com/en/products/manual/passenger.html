7. INSTALLATION OF THE PASSENGER INTO THE SEAT

1. To sit a passenger in the seat, the vehicle must necessarily be stable and immobilized. It is also recommended that the driver, helping the passenger to get in, be on the side of the stand (when you deal with a motorcycle that does not have a central stand).

2. Make sure that the passenger is firmly maintained but comfortable when you adjust the harness traps.

3. Once the harness straps have been well adjusted, make sure to cover the fasteners to avoid the passenger to free himself from the harness.

• When using the seat for a fist time with a passenger, we recommend that after one (1) kilometer, you verify all the passenger and seat retention straps to be sure that the instaliation is efficient and safe.

• Check before each trip and on as many occasions as possible the tension of the anchoring straps of the exterior shell as well as of the harness.
