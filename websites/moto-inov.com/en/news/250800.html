Here is our own Quebec genius, creator of the Ovak seat.



Seen from a different angle.
 During the course of the Mondial Moto Show, I had the pleasure of meeting Michel Martin, the creator of the Ovak seat. It is without saying that his arrival did not go unnoticed since he was accompanied by many friends and children.

All the motorcycles on display were equipped with the now famous Ovak seat. It did not take long before those were surrounded by many motorcyclists, very much intrigued by this unusual seat.

Why such a seat? Having been an avid motorcyclist over 37 years, our man (Michel) could not consider his life without a motorcycle. However, a happy event in his life brought him down to earth: a child. His paternal vocation having arrived late in life, he put himself to work. He had to find a way to solve this problem in order to combine his two passions. As with many other motorcyclists, the safety harness for children available at the time was not to his liking and for that reason he worked laboriously for 3 years on his project, the Ovak seat.

That's how a motorcycle equipped with the famous seat looks.
This famous seat is made of thermoplastic and fiberglass. It can seat comfortably and safely a passenger weighing between 25 and 100 pounds on a motorcycle or a snowmobile. Furthermore, its oval shape gives it a very high resistance to impact.

The passenger is safely attached by three solid fasteners and the cushions are very comfortable and easy to maintain. The seat itself is fixed to the motorcycle by 6 shock resistant fasteners. It can be installed on most motorcycle models.
