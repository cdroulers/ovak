30/11/00, Snowmobile Québec, Volume 26 - No. 3, November 2000


Moto-Inov International, located in Acton Vale, Quebec, Canada, has developed a back seat for youngters constructed of thermoplastic and fiberglass specifically designed to protect a child weighing from 25 to 100 pounds in a fall from a moving vehicle. In case of impact, the protective shell is automatically ejected away from the vehicle.
