ABOUT OUR ENTERPRISE

Moto-Inov International researches, develops, produces and sells products and accessories for motorcyles and snowmobiles. Moto Inov products greatly enhances the use and enjoyment of these vehicles. Ovak, the first product made by Moto-Inov, is known for increased level of safety and comfort it brings to transportation by motorcycle, snowmobile or any other recreative vehicle suitable for its installation. Founded in August 1997 by Michel Martin and Paul Quirion, Moto-Inov International has its head office in Acton Vale, Province of Quebec, Canada.
